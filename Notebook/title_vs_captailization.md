# str.title()

## Function

- Capitalizes the first character of every word in the string.

## Case Conversion

- Leaves the case of non-initial characters unchanged.

## Word Delimiter Handling

- Treats spaces, hyphens (-), underscores (\_) and similar characters as word separators.

## Input Impact

- Processes each word independently.

## Special Characters

- Non-alphabetic characters act as delimiters, but remain untouched.

## Use Case

- Formatting titles, headings or names.

# str.capitalize()

## Function

- Capitalizes only the first character of the entire string.

## Case Conversion

- Converts all other characters to lowercase.

## Word Delimiter Handling

- Does not consider word boundaries or delimiters.

## Input Impact

- Works on the entire string as a single unit.

## Special Characters

- Does not affect non-alphabetic characters.

## Use Case

- Formatting the start of a sentence.
